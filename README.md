
☕ Coffee Shop Sales Analysis | SQL
📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes a Coffee Shop sales database using SQL to uncover business insights that support strategic decision-making. By solving real-world business problems with SQL queries, the project identifies sales trends, customer behavior, product performance, and city-wise market opportunities.

🚀 Project Objectives
Analyze sales performance across multiple cities.
Identify high-performing products and customers.
Evaluate market potential using population and customer data.
Measure monthly sales growth.
Recommend the best cities for business expansion.
📂 Database Schema

The project consists of four relational tables:

City – City details, population, rent, and ranking.
Products – Coffee product information and pricing.
Customers – Customer details linked to cities.
Sales – Transaction data including sales amount, date, and product ratings.
📊 Business Problems Solved
Estimated coffee consumers based on city population.
Revenue generated across cities during the last quarter of 2023.
Best-selling coffee products by sales volume.
Average customer spending in each city.
Population vs. unique customer analysis.
Top 3 selling products in every city.
Unique coffee customers by city.
Revenue vs. rent analysis for each city.
Monthly sales growth using SQL window functions.
Recommended top 3 cities for future business expansion.
